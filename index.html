A local club for fans of Warhammer 40K to gather and organise friendly match-ups in a structured league format within the Newbury, Berkshire area.

Why join a league?

Improve your 40K tactics!

By having to play your games in a more competitive environment you force yourself to learn and improve your critical 40K thinking.

Learn by exposure!

Whether you win or lose, every game is a great teacher. As the league follows the same rules as most GT tournaments you improve your Warhammer 40K tournament knowledge without having the pressure of an official tournament.

Meet fellow 40K players!

Meet like minded individuals looking for a fun game of 40K while also helping each other improve our overall strategic gameplay and critical thinking of Warhammer 40K.

Just have fun!

This is a casual league, just have fun, share a drink, throw some dice and maybe make a friend or three while asking questions or guiding someone new into a life-long hobby!
